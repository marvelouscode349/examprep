JAMB 2023 • Question 4

If x² - 5x + 6 = 0, what are the values of x?

Your answer: DCorrect: A
AI Explanation

To solve x² - 5x + 6 = 0, find two numbers that multiply to 6 and add to -5. Those are -2 and -3, giving (x-2)(x-3)=0. Therefore x=2 OR x=3, which is option A. Option D is wrong because -3 doesn't satisfy the equation.

JAMB 2022 • Question 7

Find the derivative of y = 3x³ + 2x² - 5x + 1

Your answer: BCorrect: A
AI Explanation

Multiply coefficient by power and reduce power by 1. 3x³→9x², 2x²→4x, -5x→-5, constant disappears. Answer = 9x² + 4x - 5.
